# Physiology

## Primary adaptation

VO2max improvement.

Five-minute intervals performed slightly above FTP maximize oxygen consumption while maintaining a sustainable workload.

---

## Main physiological adaptations

- Increased stroke volume
- Higher cardiac output
- Expanded plasma volume
- Increased mitochondrial density
- Improved oxygen extraction
- Higher aerobic ceiling

---

## Energy systems

Approximate contribution

Aerobic:
≈85%

---

## Why 5-minute intervals?

Research consistently shows that intervals between four and six minutes maximize accumulated time near VO2max while maintaining high-quality power output.
